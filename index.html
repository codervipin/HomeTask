HD Revanna first member from Deve Gowda family to be sent to Jail.

posted on 09 May,2024

BENGALURU : A special court for elected representatives on Wednesday remanded HD Revana, JD(S) MLA from Holenarsispur and son of ex-PM HD Deve Gowda, in judicial custody for a week over abduction charges registered against him by KR Nagar police in Mysuru. Revana, the first member from the Deve Gowda family to be arrested and lodged in jail in a criminal case, was in tears as he stepped out of the courteroom, before being bundled off directly to the Central Jail on the southeastern fringes of the city.

Japan Grapples With 9 Million Empty Homes, More Than The Population Of New York

posted on 9 May,2024

Japan is confronting a significant problem with vacant houses, reaching a record of nine million - exceeding the population of New York City. This surge is directly linked to the country's declining population, according to a report by CNN. Traditionally, abandoned houses, known as "akiya," were found primarily in rural areas. However, the trend is spreading to major cities like Tokyo and Kyoto. This phenomenon presents a significant challenge for the government, which is already grappling with an ageing population and a low birth rate.
