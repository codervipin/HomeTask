BENGALURU: A special court for elected representatives on Wednesday remanded HD Revanna, JD(S) MLA from Holenarsispur and son of ex-PM HD Deve Gowda, in judicial custody for a week over abduction charges registered against him by KR Nagar police in Mysuru.
Revanna, the first member from the Deve Gowda family to be arrested and lodged in jail in a criminal case, was in tears as he stepped out of the courtroom, before being bundled off directly to the Central Jail on the southeastern fringes of the city.He was allotted under trial prisoner (UTP) number 4567.Ravindra Kumar B Kattimani, the additional chief metropolitan magistrate, ordered that the Holenarasipura MLA be kept in judicial custody till May 14. He will be housed in the VIP block of the prison. in Parppana Agrahara. Citing safety concerns, he has been allotted a single barrack with a dedicated bathroom and other basic facilities, not available to other under-trial prisoners.Prison officials told TOI the MLA will be lodged in the admissions room at jail for a couple of days. "This is to ensure he is free from infectious diseases. We will the move him to the relevant barrack," they said.Earlier on Wednesday, SIT produced Revanna in court as his four-day police custody ended. SIT requested the court for his judicial custody, citing non-cooperation during interrogation.
